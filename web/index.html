The mystery behind “lorem ipsum”

It isn’t random gibberish. Lorem ipsum is scrambled Latin from Cicero’s de Finibus Bonorum et Malorum (45 BC) — a treatise on pleasure and pain, where dolorem ipsum means “pain itself.” This whole toy is built on that genuine source: slide the blend toward Latin and Cicero resurfaces.
